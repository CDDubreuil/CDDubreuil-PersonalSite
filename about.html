My Life So Far

I always pictured I'd be a scientist or artist when I grew up...which didn't quite happen, but I suppose life rarely works out the way we expect. I attended the University of Central Missouri for two years as an art education major. After a few years of shuffling between odd jobs and being a mom, I've decided to try my hand at coding and see where that takes me. With development, I hope to blend my love of art and technology someday. I aspire to design video games eventually, so that I will finally be able to play exactly the games that I want.
